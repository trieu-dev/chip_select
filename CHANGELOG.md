## 0.1.0

Initial release.

- `MultiSelect` widget with chip display and live-search dropdown overlay.
- Controlled API: `selectedItems` + `onChanged(List<String>)`.
- `Form` / `FormField` integration with `validator` support.
- `label`, `hint`, `maxDropdownItems`, `controller`, and `decoration` parameters.
- Already-selected items are excluded from the dropdown automatically.
- Theming via `ColorScheme` tokens; override with `BoxDecoration` if needed.
